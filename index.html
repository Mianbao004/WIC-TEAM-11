FavorHive

FavorHive is a social network for mutual friends and the UCSD communitiy to exchange favors, such as carpooling to the airport or borrowing items. Users can post requests or offers, and members of the network can respond, thereby fostering a collaborative space of supportive networks. Our website operates similarly to a large group chat but with structured posts to track favors easily. We aim to cultivate a culture of generosity and trust that normalizes asking for favors and support.

The Motivation behind FavorHive

At FavorHive, we’re all about connecting UCSD students to make everyday life a little easier—whether it's ridesharing, borrowing items, or simply lending a hand. We understand that with busy schedules, asking for help can sometimes feel as awkward or overwhelming as trying to find a parking spot on campus. To aid this, our platform is designed to take the initial stress out of making a request while fostering a community where supporting is the norm. In doing so, we also aim to not only strengthening campus bonds but also reduce unnecessary trips and environmental impacts.
