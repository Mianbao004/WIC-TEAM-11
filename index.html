Welcome to The Favors Economy!

Favors Economy is a social network for mutual friends to exchange favors, like giving a ride to the airport or borrowing an item. Users can post requests or offers, and friends in the network can respond, creating a community of shared help. It functions like a large group chat but with structured posts to track favors easily. The goal is to make asking for and giving help simple, fostering a culture of generosity among trusted connections.

My Activism Project: Favors Economy

Organizing favors between people in the same community to save and share energy and recources.

About My Topic

People often feel uncomfortable asking friends for simple favors, this website allows for this to be done with ease, ensuring recources are pooled together rather than wasted on seperate tasks.
